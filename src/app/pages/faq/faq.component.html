Who can provide Service on Marammatwala?

Anyone who can provide quality service under different categories on Marammatwala is welcome. In order to start providing service, you need to have

  • GSTIN / Pan Card for Individual
  • Cancelled Cheque
  • Sample Signatures

How do I offer my category of service on Marammatwala?

To be a Marammatwala Service provider:

  1. Register yourself at seller.marammatwala.in.
  2. List your service under specific service category and sub category.
  3. On receiving an order, provide service either at customer place or at your own work shop. If service is provided at work shop and completed, mark it as ‘Ready to Dispatch’. Our logistics partner will pick up the serviced product and deliver it to the customer.
  4. After your order is successfully completed, Marammatwala will settle your payment within 7-15 business days based.

 

Can I offer both products and services on Marammatwala?

Currently, you can only sell services as per categories on portal and not products on Marammatwala.

Do I need to courier serviced products to Marammatwala?

No, many of service categories are to be offered at customer place. For “Insitu repair” type of service provided, it will be their own responsibility to pick-up and delivery your serviced product. Only for “Repair at Repair Shop” type of service provider, Marammatwala delivery partner will handle pick-up and delivery of your serviced products. All you need to do is pack the product and keep it ready for dispatch. Our logistics partner will pick up the product from you and deliver it to the customer or Vice-versa.

Pricing and Payments

What are the fees charged?

The following deductions are made from the order item value:

  • Referral fee: A percentage of the service order value based on category/sub-category
  • Deal Closing Fee: A slab wise fixed fee. This varies based on service Order value

What is Referral fee and how much referral fee is charged?

Referral fee is a certain percentage of the service order value. It differs across categories and sub-categories.

How and when do I get paid?

All payments are made through NEFT transactions (online banking). The payment is made directly to your bank account within the next 7-15 business days from the date of service completed.
